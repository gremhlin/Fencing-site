Competitions

For the 2018-2019 season, the St Andrews Men's First Team will compete in the British University and College Sport (BUCS) Fencing league, while the Women's First Team will compete in the Scottish 1A League.

The club also hosts competitions of our own organisation. In the past, we have run the Saints Sabre Open in the fall, the Mixed Team Competition in April and the Combat Triathalon in May. These competitions are designed to extend beyond the club, so anyone is welcome to participate. Please email us at fencing@saints-sport.com if you are inclined to find out more about any of our events.
