Fencing at St Andrews

As a club, we compete in all three weapons. We are very proud to say that anyone is welcome join the club no matter what skill level. Complete beginners can benefit from beginners sessions where current team members teach the basics of fencing. Veteran fencers who want to get involved with the club will have the opportunity to hone their technique with weekly lessons from our coach, Stan Stoodley, and compete on behalf of the University across the United Kingdom.
Frequently shortlisted by the University's Athletic Union for the award of Club of the Year, the club is a great way to make the most of your time at St Andrews. Over the course of a year of practices, bus rides to away matches, Sinners on Wednesday nights at the Student Union, you get to know everyone on the team.

Training Times

  • Mondays: Beginner Session from 8:00 to 10:30 pm
  • Wednesdays: Advanced Session from 1:30 to 2:30 pm, Open Box from 2:30 to 5:00 pm
  • Fridays: Team training from 1:00 to 2:30 pm, Beginners Session from 2:30 to 4:00 pm
  • Sundays: Team Training from 6:30 to 9:00 pm

To join, email fencing@saints-sport.com to let us know you're interested!
